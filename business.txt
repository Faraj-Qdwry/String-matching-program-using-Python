AirAsia India to commence Surat service from June

NEW DELHI, May 8 (Bernama) -- AirAsia India has added Gujarat's 
commercial city Surat to its growing network and will start daily 
flights from June 1.

"Surat is a business hub, with a dominant presence of diamond and 
textile industries, and a large number of merchants travelling out 
of Surat for various business engagements."This makes it a great 
opportunity for AirAsia India to open up the market and empower them 
to travel faster at affordable costs," the airline said in a statement.

AirAsia India is the first airline to connect Bengaluru and Surat via 
a direct service.The airline flies to 19 destinations at present using 
its hubs in Bengaluru, New Delhi and Kolkata.
